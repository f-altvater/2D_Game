Easy Mode

Enemies passing you neither deal damage
nor steal gold.
Enemies do not scale with the amount of
waves you have cleared.

Normal Mode

Enemies passing you deal some damage and
steal some gold. Enemies crashing into you
steal more gold and deal more damage.
Enemies health and damage dealt scale with
the amount of waves you have cleared.

No Mercy

Enemies passing you are treated
as if they have crashed into you.
Enemies lose less gold when killed.
You do not regenerate health after
waves, only if you get a health upgrade.

Press Spacebar to start
